Why OMK?

Not another AI wrapper. A runtime that remembers, routes, and verifies.

Memory

OMK remembers your project structure, conventions, and decisions across sessions.

Routing

Model lanes

Auto-route by task complexity. Fast models for simple edits, strong models for architecture.

Governance

Consent & DLP

Model lanes

Challenge

Quality

Premium

Benchmark and comparison lane. Bring your own API key or use Relay credits.
